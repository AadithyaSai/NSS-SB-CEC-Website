NRPF

National Resource Protection Force (NRPF) attempts to form units of environmentally conscious students in colleges who will in turn help to protect and maintain the natural resources. The main focus is to make the students environmentally sensitive. This includes two major programs traatag and thanal.

KTU CARE

KTU Care, a flagship program of the KTU NSS Cell, is dedicated to providing compassionate support and care to the elderly. Through palliative care visits and various initiatives, KTU Care aims to enhance the well-being and comfort of older individuals, ensuring they receive the attention and assistance they deserve.

RUDRASENA

Rudrasena is a flagship program, committed to bolstering blood donation initiatives and providing timely assistance to those in need. Through active engagement with blood donors and swift response to blood donation cases, Rudrasena aims to ensure a steady supply of blood and contribute to saving lives.

ENERGY CELL

The Energy Cell, a flagship initiative, is dedicated to the cause of energy conservation. Its primary focus lies in organizing energy audits conducted by volunteers. Through these audits, it promote awareness and implementation of sustainable energy practices, thereby contributing to the broader goal of resource efficiency.

About

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a Central Sector Scheme of Government of India, Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports. It provides opportunity to the student youth of 11th & 12th Class of schools at +2 Board level and student youth of Technical Institution, Graduate & Post Graduate at colleges and University level of India to take part in various Government led community service activities & programmes. The primary objective of developing the personality and character of the student youth through voluntary community service. ‘Education through Service’ is the purpose of the NSS. NSS was launched in 1969 in 37 Universities involving about 40,000 volunteers which has now spread over 657 Universities and 51 +2 Councils/Directorates, covering 20,669 Colleges/ Technical Institutions and 11,988 Senior Secondary School.
